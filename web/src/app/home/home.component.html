Situated within 1.1 km of Gregory Lake, Vilmic Lake Inn & Holiday Bungalow in Nuwara Eliya has a number of amenities including barbecue facilities and a garden. At the hotel, each room is equipped with a desk. The private bathroom is fitted with a hair dryer. Vilmic Lake Inn & Holiday Bungalow offers a terrace. You can play billiards at the accommodation. Victoria Park of Nuwara Eliya is 3.6 km from Vilmic Lake Inn & Holiday Bungalow.
